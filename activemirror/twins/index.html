Active Mirror / AI Twins

⟡ AI Twins

Four cognitive companions, each with a distinct thinking style. Choose the Twin that matches how you need to think right now.

⟡ Guardian

Role: Protects your focus and filters noise

What Guardian Does

  • Assesses if something aligns with your goals
  • Protects your time and attention from distractions
  • Helps you maintain boundaries
  • Acts as a wise advisor — direct but warm

Example Prompts

  • "Should I take on this new project?"
  • "Is this worth my attention?"
  • "What should I focus on today?"

Best For

Architects, Builders, Strategists — people who need help filtering and focusing.

◈ Scout

Role: Explores possibilities and surfaces opportunities

What Scout Does

  • Finds unexpected connections between ideas
  • Surfaces opportunities you might miss
  • Explores adjacent possibilities
  • Stays curious and adventurous

Example Prompts

  • "What am I missing here?"
  • "What adjacent areas should I explore?"
  • "Where are the hidden connections?"

Best For

Explorers, Connectors — people who thrive on discovery and new angles.

◇ Synthesizer

Role: Connects dots and builds frameworks

What Synthesizer Does

  • Finds patterns across disparate concepts
  • Builds unifying structures
  • Creates coherence from chaos
  • Weaves threads together systematically

Example Prompts

  • "How do these ideas connect?"
  • "What framework unifies this?"
  • "Help me structure this chaos"

Best For

Analysts, Creatives — people who need help integrating complex information.

◎ Mirror

Role: Reveals blind spots and challenges assumptions

What Mirror Does

  • Asks questions that reveal assumptions
  • Shows you what you might not see
  • Challenges your thinking gently
  • Acts as a trusted friend who tells hard truths

Example Prompts

  • "What am I not seeing?"
  • "What assumptions am I making?"
  • "Why am I really avoiding this?"

Best For

Scholars — people who value deep reflection and honest feedback.
