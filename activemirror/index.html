Consent & Safety

⚠️ Active Mirror is experimental AI. It is not therapy, medical advice, or a substitute for professional help. Results are for entertainment and reflection only.

Mirror Proof Protocol

Before using AI features, users must complete informed consent:

  • Full Mirror Proof — 3 acknowledgments + cryptographic hash (for AI chat)
  • Light Consent — Quick banner acknowledgment (for quizzes)

Consent is stored per-session and carries across all pages once granted.

MirrorGate Protection

All AI responses pass through MirrorGate, a safety layer that blocks harmful content before it reaches users.
